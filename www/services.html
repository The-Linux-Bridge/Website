Services

We help anyone who is interested perform a "live boot", which is essentially test-driving the Linux Operating System without modifying any of the storage devices on the machine.

We offer temporary storage options to our customers so they can easily backup their important files, photos and videos before wiping their hard drive. Our team encrypts all of the clients data and then transfers it to a sturdy external storage device.

We help our clients upgrade their operating system to a Linux distribution of their choice completely free of charge, helping them choose the best distribution for their needs.

We offer free technical support to help resolve any issues or questions that anyone may have. After the initial 3 free sessions (15 minutes each), a small charitable donation of $5 will give an extra 2 sessions (30 minutes) up to a maximum of 10 sessions per month.

As our goal is to help anyone who is interested in open source and all its benefits, if we do not have anyone waiting to use their sessions then we will happily provide assistance.

Our nonprofit introduces people to the free and open source software philosophy. We explain the benefits of using free and open source software along with sharing security and privacy practices that will help keep them safe both online and offline.
