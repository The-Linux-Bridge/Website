About

The Linux Bridge is a nonprofit organization commited to helping people make a smooth and seamless transition to open source software and systems like Linux. We are a 3DE business made up of students who are passionate about spreading the message of open source software and its many benefits, including improved security and privacy, greater efficiency and performance, and the ability to customize and control your own operating system.

To help people make the switch with confidence, we offer a range of services and resources, including live boot demonstrations, temporary storage options for data backup, free operating system upgrades, and technical support. Our team is dedicated to providing excellent customer support and guidance, and we are always here to help ensure a smooth and successful transition.

Our mission is to help as many people as possible make the transition to open source software and systems, and to promote the use of open source software as a key part of building a more secure, efficient, and private online and offline environment. We believe that open source software is an essential part of creating a better future and world for everyone, and we are dedicated to spreading the message of open source software and helping people understand and appreciate the options they have when it comes to using technology.
